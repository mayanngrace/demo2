A student community platform built for Iskolar ng Bayan. Post tasks, offer services, connect with fellow students, and stay on top of your academic life — all in one place.

Our Mission

CampusConnect was created to empower students with a simple, fast, and accessible hub for campus life. We believe in open collaboration, academic transparency, and building a stronger Iskolar community — one post at a time.
